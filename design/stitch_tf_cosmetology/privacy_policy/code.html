Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2024 | Document Ref: AL-2024-PRIV-01

At Apothecary Lab, we believe that data integrity is as fundamental as ingredient purity. This policy outlines our commitment to transparency and the rigorous protocols we employ to safeguard your personal information within our digital clinical environment.

01.

Information Collection

We collect information that is essential for the formulation and delivery of our bespoke cosmetological services. This includes identifiable metrics provided during account creation, clinical skin profiles, and purchase histories.

When you interact with our laboratory interface, we automatically gather technical data such as IP addresses, browser specifications, and session durations to optimize our science-first user experience.

02.

Use of Data

Data utilization is strictly confined to the enhancement of your aesthetic journey. We utilize your profile to:

  • biotech Refine product formulations based on aggregated skin-health trends.
  • verified Validate compliance with regional cosmetic manufacturing regulations.
  • science Facilitate clinical-grade communication regarding your active orders and treatment plans.
03.

Cookies and Tracking

Our digital infrastructure employs subtle tracking technologies, termed "cookies," to maintain the state of your laboratory session. These small data fragments allow our system to remember your preferences and cart selections.

You may configure your browser to reject these persistent identifiers; however, please note that certain high-precision features of the Apothecary Lab interface may become unavailable.

04.

Third Party Disclosure

Apothecary Lab maintains a strictly closed-loop ecosystem. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to external marketing entities.

Information is only shared with trusted clinical partners and logistics providers who assist us in operating our manufacturing laboratory and delivering your treatments, provided they adhere to our non-disclosure standards.

05.

Your Rights

You retain full sovereignty over your clinical record. Under applicable data protection laws, you possess the right to:

Access & Portability

Request a comprehensive export of your personal data currently stored in our archives.

Erasure

Petition for the permanent removal of your clinical profile from our active manufacturing systems.

Regulatory Inquiry

Should you require further clarification regarding our data handling protocols or wish to exercise your rights, please contact our Compliance Officer.
